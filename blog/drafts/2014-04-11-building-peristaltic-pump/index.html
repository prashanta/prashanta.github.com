Experiences from Building a Peristaltic Pump

Peristaltic pump utilizes the action known as peristalsis -contraction and relaxation of flexible tube to transport fluid. Unlike other pumps, fluid that is being pumped does not come in contact with any other part of the pump except the tube through which it is being transported. This presents a unique use case.
